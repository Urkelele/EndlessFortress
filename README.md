Endless Fortress is a roguelike with endless runner and clicker mechanics. The user plays as one of the available characters inspired by medieval fantasy, and ventures into a dark fortress to gather treasures, improve their abilities and see how far they can get.
On the runner part, the player hast to avoid obstacles, collect gold and items, and choose the path forward on the fortress. The running parts end when the player reaches either a safe spot, a shop, an event or an encounter with enemies. On the encounters, the player will fight the enemies by choosing the one to target and using any activated abilities it might have, the character will attack the targeted enemy automatically. These two sides of gameplay interconnect directly, following one after the other cyclically. Both parts of the gameplay will be affected by the character chosen and the items gathered throughout the run.
